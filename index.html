Overview

RORMJS

Rigs of Rods Monster Jam Shows brings an immersive and highly realistic Monster Jam experience to Rigs of Rods. Featuring custom-designed trucks, tracks, and arenas, each show captures the excitement, intensity, and spectacle of live Monster Jam events. With original graphics, overlays, commentary, and creative presentation, these shows provide our fans and viewers with a one-of-a-kind simulation that combines the thrill of Monster Jam competition in Rigs of Rods.
