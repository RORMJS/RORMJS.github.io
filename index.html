Overview

RORMJS

Rigs Of Rods Monster Jam Shows is a fan-made project that focuses on creating extremely realistic Monster Jam shows in Rigs of Rods. It features custom events, schedules, lineups, commentary, and presentation designed to capture the excitement, energy, and spectacle of Monster Jam events. The project provides fans with an immersive and authentic simulation experience. It is not endorsed, sponsored, or affiliated with Feld Entertainment or Monster Jam®.
